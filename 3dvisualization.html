3D.VISUALIZATION

In the last eight years AZVA has developed several techniques to achieve its current portfolio. With experience in many different animation and rendering packages, from proprietary to open-source, always with the customer in mind. (We believe that more important than the process in itself, it is the end product that deserves all the attention.) We focus the best end product to deliver to the client.

AZVA can achieve images within various project stages. Our purpose is to deliver beautiful and communicating images, and, as such, all we need to know is what the client wishes to communicate. We can build 3D models from scratch, or work with in-studio developed 3D models. We can also pick up a project on its primary stage and develop architectural language in order to communicate the idea to a client or a competition jury. As a group of architects, AZVA takes advantage of understanding better the clients needs than an illustrator could do. AZVA builds you images.
