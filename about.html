About AZVAvisuals

Architecture is our passion; and so is communicating through images. We also love interaction, together with computers and programming. So we made up AZVA: a company dedicated to communicate architecture through visuals.

Our history comes a long way. In 2001 a partnership was funded in university while studying architecture called rucativa. This group started outsourcing various works to architecture offices while simultaneously creating architecture through the participation in competitions and the development of small projects. Few years later, rucativa started delivering 3d visualizations as well as webdesigns. With a growing amount of work, much of them across borders, it was time to let go this outsourcing potential to become a company of its own. Thus, AZVA came into existence.

Our scope is global. Our lexicon is architecture, and our media is graphics. We are currently based in Porto, Portugal, and we develop works internationally. Digital art has logistic advantages, and the internet empowers them. We believe in a world where the internet plays an equalizing and bonding role, getting different people from different countries and cultures together, reaching further in better solutions.
