mag - arquitectura &urbanismo

The core of this design is based on its interactive tag system. With this system, one can effectively filter the projects until it is found. Multiple tag selections are permitted which, along with the animation, make the navigation very intuitive.

Another point to be considered is the verticality of the website. The search for the direct understanding of construtive technology by this architecture office is translated in the understanding of web browsing as a native technology. Scrolling is part of the web, and the most efficient way to browse content. This webdesign is a bit of a homage to this system.
