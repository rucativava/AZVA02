Bragança territorial and urban analysis

Illustrations produced for the article "Bragança, cidade-fortaleza "setuada no estremo de portugall e Castela" published in scientific magazine Monumentos nº 32

The terrain shading was produced inhouse with 3d rendering software recurring to altimetric data delivered by NASA. All the roads and shading were also graphic work, making some of these unique maps. We also layered various historic imagery using digital techniques in order to help the investigation.

Original investigation by Assistant Professor Maria José Casanova, University of Porto.
