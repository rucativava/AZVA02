TRAINING

As AZVA came on building its portfolio, the demand for advices and teaching grew over time. After some exeriences, we found out that organizing specificic courses brings much value to the learning experience. Be it intensive crash-courses, learning over time, or one-themed workshops, AZVA has the necessary know-how in various digital techniques and softwares that can be valuable to the learner.

Our courses are, as a starting point, mainly on manipulating specific software suites. Architecture is the main activity in which our learners dwell. Nevertheless, all that is needed is the urge to learn and produce results.

For the time being, we specialize in BIM, and in 3D rendering. Advanced 3D modelling, graphics editing and advanced spreadsheeting are other areas of learning which we strongly believe its values in the AEC industry. We also value the Mac OS X platform, although all the softwares we teach are also available for the Windows operating system.

Target Audience

We organize courses within company environment, as well as self-organized.

You can hire us to train your personnel in a specific software or workflow, and the training sessions will be performed in your own company, using your everyday tools - the computers and software. We have partnerships with entities who help companies get access to development funds and sponsor training investments.

We also create training events pertaining to what the architecture professionals and students might need. We cover mainly two aspects: BIM modellings and 3D Visualization. On a parallel way, graphics editing, imaging and complex 3d modelling are areas of knowledge that go side-by-side.
