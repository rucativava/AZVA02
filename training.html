TRAINING

We specialize in BIM, and in 3D rendering. Advanced 3D modeling, graphics editing and advanced spreadsheeting are other areas of learning which we strongly believe on its values to the AEC industry. We also value the Mac OS X platform, although all the softwares we teach are also available for Windows. We focus on the area of architectural graphics.

The workshops can be intensive crash-courses, learning over time, or bespoke workshops, AZVA has the necessary know-how in various digital techniques and softwares that can be valuable to the learner.

Target Audience

Our courses are targeted to companies or to individuals.

You can hire us to train your staff in a specific software or workflow, and the training sessions will be performed in your own company, using your everyday tools - the computers and software. We have partnerships with institutions that help companies be elegible for fundings in training investments.

We also create training events suitable to what the architecture professionals and students might need. We cover mainly two aspects: BIM modeling and 3D visualization. On parallel, graphics editing, imaging and complex 3D modeling are areas of knowledge that go side-by-side.
